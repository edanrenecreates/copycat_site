'osteopathie'

Osteopathy is a manual therapy which complaints are resolved to continue searching for the cause of the complaint and that address. Everything in the human body is mobile, can move and must move. That is the main point of Osteopathy. Because of influences from inside and the outside can be disrupted that possibility to move. This is for example by injuries, a fall or accident, scars after surgery, overload or stress. When the disturbance is too large or is to long, the body is unable to completely repair it yourself.


When I trained osteopath to examine the various systems within the body motion losses and thus to find a permanent solution to your problems.

Our Process

Investigation

During the interview, I will conduct an extensive interview with you about the complaint, medical history and lifestyle habits. This is to get clear to me that the cause of the complaint is.


Then follows a comprehensive physical examination. Here I look at the motion losses throughout the body. Because it does not have to be the cause of the pain is in the same place as where there is pain. The intake takes 60 minutes.

Through my training I am able to recognize symptoms that require further investigation. When this is the case I will refer you to the doctor or specialist.

Therapy

During the treatment, I will restore the body's movement with my hands. A treatment lasts for 45 min. The follow-up treatment will take place after one or several weeks. So the body has a chance to repair itself.


Acute symptoms may be remedied within three to four treatments. With longstanding complaints will show up completely may last longer and often require a longer course of treatment.


After 3 to 4 treatments, always evaluates whether there is a positive response to the treatment. If not so then we see whether further treatment is useful.

Zero Complaints

During the osteopathic treatment, I look for cause of the symptoms then this fix.


When certain movement losses are present for a long time, it is possible that yet again loses its mobility the fabric after a certain period of time.


In such cases it is advisable to come every six months or year to walk again after everything. This is to prevent loss return movement, with the associated symptoms.
